About Me

My name is Conner Dejecacion, and I'm a journalist and tabletop game designer from Frisco, Texas. I'm currently a graduate journalism student at Northwestern University. I received my undergraduate degrees in Journalism and English Literature from Northwestern in 2025.

I have a passion for gaming and have channeled it into my journalistic work. In the winter of 2022, I worked as an editorial intern at DBLTAP, a website that covers the realms of gaming and eSports. In April of 2023 I became a freelance contributor with DBLTAP and its sister site, AppTrigger, under the umbrella of Minute Media.

In the spring of 2024 I worked with Telescope Film as an editorial intern, working on a comprehensive database of foreign films and curating exemplary works to promote them with American audiences.

In Fall 2024 and Winter 2025, I served as the editor-in-chief of North by Northwestern, campus' undergraduate magazine. I oversaw the launch of its new website in January 2025. Since then, I've done work for Stranger's Guide, a world culture magazine.

As a journalist, I aspire to champion interactive media as an art form and hope to bring nuanced analysis and criticism to the forefront of games media, while also offering textured coverage of the ever-evolving industry.
